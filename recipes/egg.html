Egg

The picture shown above depicts a cooked egg

Ingredients

    Stove
    Egg
    Oil
    Cooking pan
    Spatula
    Serving plate
    Eating utensil

Steps

    1. Turn on stove on medium heat
    2. Place pan on top of flame
    3. Pour 3 teaspoons of oil on pan
    4. Let oil heat up until it starts splashing
    5. Grab 1 uncooked egg
    6. Carefully break & open egg on top of pan with oil
    7. Make sure that no shells fall in pan
    8. Repeat steps 5-7 to your liking
    9. Move eggs around with spatula so they don't stick to pan & burn
    10. Cook eggs until they are no longer raw
    11. Once eggs are cooked, pour eggs on serving plate
    12. Grab eating utensil & enjoy!
